# Design System: VendiCovoit
**Project ID:** 5171875962400351422

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Atmosphere: Editorial Hospitality**

The VendiCovoit design system is designed to feel professional, trustworthy, and organic. It moves away from the clinical brightness of traditional tech apps, embracing a "Creamy Paper" foundation that reduces eye strain and feels more like a high-end travel editorial. The aesthetic is minimalist yet warm, prioritizing large image assets and clear, high-contrast typography.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Forest Green (#006a45):** The primary brand anchor. Used for high-intent actions, primary buttons, and critical branding moments. It signals growth, safety, and stability.
*   **Creamy Parchment (#f5f2ea):** The core background surface. A warmer, more sophisticated alternative to pure white that provides an organic, premium feel.
*   **Graphite Onyx (#1b1c1c):** The primary data color. Used for maximum legibility in body text and headlines against the parchment surface.
*   **Clear Sky Blue (#4285f4):** The accent color. Used for non-primary highlights, links, and secondary interactive elements like the "Share" or "Install" prompts.
*   **Radical Rose (#ff385c):** The error and alert color. Used for destructive actions (e.g., "Cancel booking") and warning states.
*   **Soft Sage Outline (#bec9c0):** Used for subtle borders and separators, maintaining the organic feel without harsh digital lines.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family:** **Inter** is used for both Display and Body text, ensuring functional clarity and a modern, neutral voice.
*   **Weights:**
    *   **Black (900):** Reserved for primary headlines and major section titles to create a strong editorial hierarchy.
    *   **Bold (700):** Used for subheaders and primary button labels.
    *   **Medium (500):** Used for body text and labels to maintain readability without feeling too heavy.
*   **Styling:** Titles often use tight letter-spacing to feel more authoritative.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:**
    *   **Primary:** Deep Forest Green background with white text. Generously rounded (16px–24px) to feel friendly and modern.
    *   **Secondary:** Soft background (gray-100 or brand-on-surface/5) with Graphite text. Same roundness.
*   **Cards/Containers:**
    *   **Style:** Generously rounded corners (24px - 40px). 
    *   **Background:** Typically pure White or Creamy Parchment.
    *   **Depth:** Whisper-soft diffused shadows or no shadows at all, using background color shifts to define hierarchy.
*   **Modals:** Utilize a backdrop blur (blur-sm) with an 80% opacity overlay to keep focus on the information while maintaining visual context.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Density:** Spacious and breathable. Uses wide margins (p-6 to p-8) to create an "uncluttered" premium feel.
*   **Rounding Consistency:** High roundness values are applied consistently across all interactive elements (buttons, cards, inputs) to create a soft, inviting interface.
*   **Asymmetry:** Headlines and content are often left-aligned with strategic whitespace to mimic the layout of a luxury magazine.
